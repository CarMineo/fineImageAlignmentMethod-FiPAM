related to the paper titled: "Fine Alignment of Thermographic Images for
Robotic Inspection of Parts with Complex Geometries", by C. Mineo,
N. Montinaro, M. Fustaino, A. Pantano and D. Cerniglia.

INSTRUCTIONS TO USE THE DEMONSTRATION SCRIPT:
In order to demonstrate the Fine Pixel-based Alignment Method (FiPAM),
the present script is provided with the dataset of thermographic images
presented in the journal paper. This script executes FiPAM and plots
the results.
